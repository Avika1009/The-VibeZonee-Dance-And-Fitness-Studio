Meet Our Founder

"Movement is the universal language of the soul. I built this space for you to speak it freely."

Hi, I’m Aabha Bhaagwat, founder of The VIBEZONEe. My journey is a blend of science and soul; I hold an M.Sc. in Microbiology and an M.Tech in Biotechnology, but my true calling has always been dance and fitness. As a Tiara Mrs. India 2019 Winner, I learned that confidence and discipline can truly transform lives & values I now pour into every corner of my studio.

With years of experience as a certified Dance and Zumba instructor, I created The VIBEZONEe with a vision to build more than just a fitness studio. I wanted to create a space where people of all ages feel energetic, confident, strong, and truly themselves.Here, every class is designed with love, positivity, and high energy. Whether you come to dance, get fit, relieve stress, or simply enjoy yourself. My mission is to help every member discover their inner vibe and shine brighter every day. Come join me and experience fitness with fun, passion, and transformation!

— Aabha Bhaagwat
